PS5: Horizon Forbidden West

Score: 9/10

Price on PS Store: $49.99

Horizon Forbidden West is an epic open-world action RPG that continues the story of Aloy in a post-apocalyptic world filled with robotic creatures. The gameplay is engaging, with exploration, combat, and puzzle-solving elements that keep you hooked for hours.

Pros:

  • Stunning visuals and world design
  • Deep and immersive storyline
  • Varied combat mechanics

Cons:

  • Some side quests feel repetitive
  • Steep learning curve for new players

Overall, it's a must-play for PS5 owners looking for an adventure.
